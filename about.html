ABOUT THE WEBSITE

The team conducted a female directors analysis project for Dr. Rini Mehta at UIUC. We collected data about the 83 directors who directed the top 100 all time films. The main focus of our analyses was on gender differences and race in their films. More specifically, we conducted analysis on film languages, director nationalities, African American female directors, actor director relations as well as gender differences over time, per director and per media.
